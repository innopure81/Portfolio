Do I have to keep paper receits for taxes? The answer is No. The IRS does not require original paper receipts in the event of an audit. In fact, the IRS has advocated for “electronic storage systems” for tax-related documents since 1997. With the advent of smartphones and easily accessible file hosting services, the solution is more practical than ever.
